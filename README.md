## 🛠 Features

This application stores your knowledge in standard HTML files. Your data remains yours. You do not depend on a cloud service to view or edit your work.

### Three Editing Modes
You choose how you want to work.
* WYSIWYG: Write as you see. It looks like a final document while you type.
* Source: Edit the raw code for full control over your formatting.
* Render iframe: Preview how your note looks while you keep the editor open.

### Local-First Storage
Your files live on your computer. You keep full control over your privacy. The application uses end-to-end encryption for sync tasks. This protects your data from unauthorized access.

### AI Integration
The AI gateway helps you draft content or summarize long documents. It works as an assistant within your workflow. You decide when to trigger these features.

### Plugin System
Sandboxed plugins add extra power to your notes. You can enable a calendar view to track dates or a knowledge graph to see connections between files. The system isolates these plugins to ensure your main application stays fast and stable.

### Web Clipper
Save content from the internet directly into your library. The clipper converts messy web pages into clean notes stored in your local folder.

## 🖥 System Requirements

Your computer must meet these basic needs:
* Operating System: Windows 10 or Windows 11.
* Memory: 4GB RAM is the minimum for smooth performance.
* Storage: 200MB of free disk space for the app installation.
* Browser: Any modern web browser like Chrome, Edge, or Firefox to view your exported files.

## 📂 Managing Your Files

Your notes are standard HTML files. You can find them in your Documents folder by default. You can move, rename, or copy these files just like any photo or text document. Because they are native HTML, you open them by double-clicking. Any browser will show your notes exactly as they appeared in the editor.

## 🛡 Security and Sync

Your privacy stays protected. The end-to-end encryption process scrambles your data before it leaves your device for sync tasks. Only you hold the keys to read this information. You can use local sync methods if you prefer to keep your data off the internet entirely.

## 🤝 Troubleshooting

* Installation fails: Make sure you have enough disk space and proper permissions on your computer. Run the installer as an administrator if necessary.
* App does not launch: Check your firewall settings. The app requires local permissions to create and read files in your chosen storage folder.
* Plugin errors: Disable third-party plugins one by one to find the source of the conflict. Update the application to the latest version to ensure full compatibility.
* File access: If you move your note files, the application might ask you to re-link your library folder. Point the application to the new location to restore your notes.
